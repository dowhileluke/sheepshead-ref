# Frog

* Players: 3
* Style: Ace-Ten trick taking
* Bidding: immediate hold
* Direction: clockwise
* 36 Cards: ATKQJ987
* Hand size: 11
* Farbzwang + Trumpfzwang

## Bids

| Bid | Description | Value
|-|-|-
| Frog | Hearts trump, bidder uses blind. | 1
| Better | Non-hearts trump, no blind. | 2
| Best | Hearts trump, no blind. | 3

## Scoring

The bidder subtracts 60 from the value of all cards they captured in tricks, then multiplies the difference by the contract value. 

A positive value is collected from each opponent, and a negative value is paid to each.
